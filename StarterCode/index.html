Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

This is an interactive chart to show the correlation between 2 sets of health factors. The data set is based on 2014 ACS 1-year estimates.

Interactive plot shows different data views. To see the relationship between different factors chose the particular axis.
If we look at the "Household Income" vs "Lacks Healthcare", there is a positive correlation between them. When the household income is higher, the higher the percentage of lack in health care, with some outliers like Texas and Massachusett.

You may hover on the circles to reveal the State Name and the corresponding data.
